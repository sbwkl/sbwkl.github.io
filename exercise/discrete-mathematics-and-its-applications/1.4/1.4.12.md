Let Q(x) be the statement "x + 1 > 2x." If the domain consists of all integers, what are these truth values?

1. Q(0)
1. Q(-1)
1. Q(1)
1. $$\exist x Q(x)$$
1. $$\forall x Q(x)$$
1. $$\exist x \neg Q(x)$$
1. $$\forall x \neg Q(x)$$

Solution

1. true
1. true
1. false
1. true
1. false
1. true
1. false
