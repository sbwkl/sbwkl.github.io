What are the truth values of these statements?

1. $$\exist !x P(x) \rightarrow \exist x P(x)$$
2. $$\forall x P(x) \rightarrow \exist !x P(x)$$
3. $$\exist !x \neg P(x) \rightarrow \neg \forall x P(x)$$

Solution

1. true
2. true or false
3. true
