Let P(x) be the statement "x = x^2." If the domain consists of the integers, what are these truth values?

1. P(0)
1. P(1)
1. P(2)
1. P(-1)
1. $$\exist x P(x)$$
1. $$\forall x P(x)$$

Solution

1. true
1. true
1. false
1. false
1. true
1. false
